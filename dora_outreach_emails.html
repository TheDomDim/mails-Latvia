Segment hooks

EXEC
How leadership is actually making sense of DORA
— top-down, delegated, or grey zone. Varies by
growth stage and shapes what tooling makes sense.

TECH
Where implementation gets painful — Register of
Information, third-party mapping, or something
upstream. The gap between the regulation and
operationalising it is what we're building around.

COMPLIANCE
Hard part usually isn't interpreting the reg —
it's collecting the right data across business
lines. Aware it's a generalisation; their answer
directly informs what we build.

LEGAL
How legal is positioned internally — driving
DORA, supporting it, or pulled in reactively.
Varies more than expected; shapes what good
tooling looks like.

OTHER
Regulation hits differently by business model,
size, compliance stage. We want what we build
to reflect that variety.
