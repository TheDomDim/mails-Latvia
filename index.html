Partner structure (law firms, advisories)

Hi [FIRST NAME],

Thanks for joining our DORA webinar last month.

I imagine you're fielding a lot of DORA questions
from your clients — I'm curious what the recurring
sticking points are from where you sit. We're trying
to understand the gap between what the regulation
demands and what firms can actually deliver.
I suspect you have a clearer view than most.

Would be useful to compare notes — not sure yet
what a collaboration could look like, but I'd
rather have that conversation than not.
Worth a call?

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
CEO, Copla

Segment hooks

EXEC  How leadership is making sense of DORA —
top-down, delegated, or grey zone. Varies by
growth stage; shapes what tooling makes sense.

TECH  Where implementation gets painful — Register
of Information, third-party mapping, or
something upstream. That gap is what we build around.

COMPLIANCE  Hard part usually isn't interpreting
the reg — it's collecting data across business
lines. Their answer directly informs what we build.

LEGAL  How legal is positioned — driving DORA,
supporting it, or pulled in reactively.
Shapes what good tooling looks like.

OTHER  Regulation hits differently by size/model.
We want what we build to reflect that variety.
